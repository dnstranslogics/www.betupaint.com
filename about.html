About Betupaint

15+ Years of Excellence
Who We Are

Transforming India's Homes Since 2010

At Betupaint, we believe that every wall tells a story. Founded in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize the painting industry in India, we've grown from a small team of passionate craftsmen to a nationwide network of certified painting professionals.

We partner with India's top paint brands — Asian Paints, Berger, Nerolac, Opus, and Dulux — to bring you authorized products at transparent prices. Our mechanized, dust-free processes ensure a flawless finish every time, backed by our 1-year comprehensive service warranty.

Today, with over 10,000 satisfied clients across 50+ cities, Betupaint stands as one of India's most trusted names in premium painting services.
